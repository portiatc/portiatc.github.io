About

Built on a Decade of Relationships

Portia Training & Consulting was founded by Paulina, who spent over 10 years building deep, trust-based relationships with the global tech community through her work with GeeCON — one of Europe's leading software development conferences.

Those years of organising events, curating speaker line-ups, and collaborating with the world's top technologists gave Paulina a unique network and an eye for matching the right expert with the right challenge. Portia exists to make that network accessible to companies looking for real impact — not generic consultancy.
